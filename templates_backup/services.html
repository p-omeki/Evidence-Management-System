Services of Our Evidence Management System

1. Case Registration

Digitally register new cases quickly and efficiently, capturing all necessary details.

2. Evidence Collection

Efficiently collect and upload various types of evidence related to registered cases.

3. Evidence Storage

Securely store all collected evidence in a digital format, ensuring easy accessibility and retrieval.

4. Evidence Analysis

Analyze collected evidence using built-in tools to extract valuable insights and information.

5. Evidence Presentation

Present analyzed evidence in a structured and understandable manner, facilitating court proceedings.

6. Case Disposal

Efficiently dispose of cases, including the option to move them to a recycle bin for a defined period.

7. Recycle Bin Management

Manage disposed cases in a recycle bin, allowing for restoration within a specified timeframe.

8. Case Restoration

Restore cases from the recycle bin in case further actions need to be taken on previously disposed cases.

9. Audit Trail

Maintain a comprehensive audit trail of all actions performed within the system for accountability and transparency.

10. User Authentication and Authorization

Implement robust user authentication and authorization mechanisms to ensure secure access to the system.

11. Notifications

Provide notifications and alerts for important events, such as new case registrations, evidence analysis completion, etc.

12. Reporting

Generate detailed reports and summaries for cases, evidence, and system activities to aid in decision-making and analysis.

13. Collaboration

Facilitate collaboration among users, allowing for the sharing of information and insights related to cases and evidence.

14. Data Encryption

Ensure the security of sensitive information through data encryption to protect against unauthorized access.

15. Mobile Accessibility

Enable access to the system from mobile devices, providing flexibility and convenience for users in the field.
