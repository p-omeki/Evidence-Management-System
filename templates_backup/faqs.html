Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is the purpose of the Evidence Management System?

The system is designed to digitize and streamline the process of recording, managing, and analyzing evidence in a legal context. It aims to replace traditional paper-based methods with a more efficient and secure digital solution.

2. How do I register a new case in the system?

To register a new case, navigate to the "Register Case" section on the homepage. You will be prompted to enter relevant case details, victim information, and additional information as needed.

3. Can I upload various types of evidence?

Yes, the system supports the collection and upload of various types of evidence, including documents, images, videos, and more. You can attach relevant files when registering a case or adding evidence to an existing case.

4. How is evidence analyzed in the system?

The system provides built-in tools for evidence analysis. Analysts can use these tools to extract valuable insights and information from the collected evidence, aiding in the investigative process.

5. What happens to disposed cases?

Disposed cases are moved to a recycle bin where they remain for a specified period (e.g., 90 days). After this period, they are automatically deleted from the system. Users also have the option to restore disposed cases within the recycle bin timeframe.

6. How is user access controlled in the system?

The system implements user authentication and authorization mechanisms to control access. Users need to log in with their credentials, and their access permissions are determined by their roles and responsibilities.

7. Can I collaborate with other users on a case?

Yes, the system supports collaboration among users. You can share information, insights, and updates related to cases and evidence with other authorized users within the system.

8. Is there a mobile app for the Evidence Management System?

Currently, the system is accessible through web browsers. However, we are considering the development of a mobile app for added flexibility and convenience in the future.

9. How is sensitive information secured in the system?

The system employs data encryption to ensure the security of sensitive information. This helps protect against unauthorized access and maintains the confidentiality of case and evidence details.

10. Can I receive notifications for important events?

Yes, the system provides notification features to alert users about important events such as new case registrations, evidence analysis completion, and other relevant updates.
